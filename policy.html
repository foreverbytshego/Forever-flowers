Our Policies

We want you to shop with complete confidence. Please read our policies below.

📦 Shipping Policy

We take great care to pack and dispatch every order promptly and securely. Please read the following information about how your order will be delivered.

  • Orders are processed within 2–3 business days of payment confirmation.
  • Standard shipping within South Africa typically takes 5–7 business days.
  • Express shipping options may be available at checkout — please contact us to arrange.
  • Shipping is calculated at checkout based on your location and the size of your order.
  • You will receive a tracking number via email once your order has been dispatched.
  • We are not responsible for delays caused by courier services, public holidays, or force majeure events.

For any shipping queries, please contact us at hello@foreverflowers.co.za.

🔄 Returns & Refunds Policy

Your satisfaction is our priority. If there is an issue with your order, we will do everything we can to make it right.

  • Returns are accepted within 7 days of delivery, provided the item is unused and in its original condition and packaging.
  • To initiate a return, please contact us by email with your order number and the reason for the return.
  • Items that have been used, altered, or damaged by the customer are not eligible for return.
  • Shipping costs for returns are the responsibility of the customer, unless the item arrived damaged or incorrect.
  • Refunds will be processed within 5–10 business days of receiving the returned item.
  • We do not offer exchanges at this time, but we are happy to assist you find the right product before purchase.

If your item arrived damaged or you received the wrong product, please send us a photo and your order number within 48 hours of delivery and we will resolve it as quickly as possible.

🔒 Privacy Policy

Forever Flowers by Tshego is committed to protecting your personal information. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and how we keep it safe.

What we collect:

  • Your name, email address, and delivery address when you place an order.
  • Payment information processed securely through PayPal — we do not store card details.
  • General browsing data such as pages visited (collected anonymously via site analytics, if enabled).

How we use your information:

  • To process and fulfil your order.
  • To communicate with you about your purchase, shipping updates, or queries.
  • To improve our website and customer experience.
  • We will never sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Third-party services:

  • We use PayPal to process payments. Your payment details are handled by PayPal and subject to their privacy policy.
  • We use GitHub Pages to host this website. No personal data is stored on our servers.

You have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information at any time. Please contact us at hello@foreverflowers.co.za to make such a request.

This policy was last updated in 2025 and may be revised from time to time. Continued use of this site constitutes acceptance of our current policy.
